How It Works
Simple 6-Step Workflow

From morning stock entry to end-of-day report — everything handled automatically.

1
Add Stock Purchased
Enter items you bought from the supplier — name, quantity, purchase price. Done in 30 seconds.
2
Record Sales
Tap product → enter quantity → confirm. Works offline too. Inventory updates instantly.
3
Auto Stock Update
Stock levels adjust automatically. No manual counting. Low-stock alerts sent to your phone.
4
View Dashboard
See today's sales, remaining stock, and which products are moving fastest — all in one screen.
5
Real-Time Profit
Profit is calculated every second. Daily, weekly, monthly views with charts you can actually read.
6
Download Reports
End of day — download PDF or Excel report. Share with your accountant or save for GST filing.
